List of Automobiles and Associated Tax Rates – 2022 Model Year

From: Canada Revenue Agency

The following is a list of 2022 model year automobiles and the associated rates of excise tax on fuel-inefficient vehicles. This list is provided for reference purposes only and is not intended to be definitive and all-inclusive. Automobiles not appearing on this list may still be subject to the excise tax on fuel-inefficient vehicles. 

Please note that for excise tax purposes, the fuel consumption ratings are based on 2-cycle testing.

Legend

Model

AWD = All-wheel drive (automobile designed to operate with all wheels powered)
4WD/4x4 = Four-wheel drive (automobile designed to operate with either two wheels or four wheels powered)
FFV = Flexible-fuel vehicle (automobile designed to operate on gasoline and ethanol blends up to 85% ethanol)

Engine size
Total displacement of all cylinders (in litres [L])

Transmission/Number of gears (1 to 10)
A = Automatic
AM = Automatic manual
AS = Automatic with select shift
AV = Continuously variable
M = Manual

Fuel type
X = Regular gasoline
Z = Premium gasoline
D = Diesel
