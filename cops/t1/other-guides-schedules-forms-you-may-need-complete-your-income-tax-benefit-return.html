Other guides, schedules and forms you may need to complete your income tax and benefit return

Generally, you should use the income tax package for the province or territory where you resided on December 31.

Depending on your tax situation, you may need to refer to other guides or complete additional schedules or forms that have more detailed information.

If you entered an amount on any of the following lines, see the applicable guides, schedules and other forms.
