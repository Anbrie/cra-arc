By phone:
Change your business address by phone
Before you call

To verify your identity, you'll need:

  • your business number
  • your business name
  • your complete business address
  • your business account open or an assessed return, notice of assessment, reassessment, or other tax document

If you are calling the CRA on behalf of someone else, you must be an authorized representative.

By mail or fax:
Change your business address by mail or fax

Write a letter to include:

  • your signature
  • your business name
  • your business number (BN)
  • your old address
  • your new address
  • the effective date of the change
  • the CRA program accounts that need to be updated with the new address (or indicate if all accounts should be updated)
  • if changing more than one address related to your business, indicate all addresses on file that need to be changed.

Send your letter by mail or by fax to your tax centre. To find the information of your tax centre, go to Find a CRA address.
