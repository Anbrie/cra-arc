Get your employee's TD1 Forms

Once your employee provided their SIN, you must get the federal and provincial TD1 Forms to determine the amount of tax to be deducted from an individual's employment income or other income, such as pension income.

You may need to get additional information or forms from your employee depending on the employment situation.

Which forms to use

Situation: Employee is paid by commission

TD1X for employees paid by commission

Situation: Employee is paid by commission and lives in Quebec

TD1X and TP-1015.R.13.1-V for employees paid by commission and lives in Quebec

Situation: Employee is a fisher

TD3F for employee that are fishers

Situation: Employee is an Indian

TD1-IN for employees that are Indians
