Contact the Charities Directorate

Online:

Sign into My Business Account (MyBA) and select "Update registered charity or RCAAA information". Then select "submit an enquiry" from the dropdown menu.

Sign in to My Business Account Register
By phone:
Contact by phone
Before you call

To verify your identity, you'll need:

  • your business number
  • your business name
  • your complete business address
  • your business account open or an assessed return, notice of assessment, reassessment, or other tax document

If you are calling the CRA on behalf of someone else, you must be an authorized representative.
