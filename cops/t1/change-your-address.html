Change an address for someone else by mail

You may also change the address of another individual at the same time, such as your spouse or common-law partner. Include a signed letter with your form that contains the individual's:

  • name
  • social insurance number
  • signature

Change a business address

Notify the CRA of changes in any of your business addresses.

Different types of business addresses
  • Physical address: This is the business or head office address. It is where day-to-day activities take place. It must be the physical location of your business. It must not have a RR number or PO box. If there is no street address, use the legal description of the business location. For example, Lot 1, Concession 2.
  • Mailing address: This can be different from the physical address. For example, you may have your business mail delivered to your home or your accountant.
  • Books and records address: If your business books and records are at a different location from your physical address, you must provide this information to the CRA.
  • Different mailing addresses for each of your registered business accounts: If you have different mailing addresses for your registered businesses, you or your representative must notify us of the change of address.
