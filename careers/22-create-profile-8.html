Releasing your profile allows all hiring managers to view candidate profile information in accordance with the Data Privacy Statement. If you choose not to release your candidate profile information, it will be viewed by hiring managers associated with an application submission.

Data Privacy Statement

Provision of personal information requested on this website is required to assess your application for employment as required by section 54 of the CRA Act. Your personal information will be used to confirm your eligibility for employment with the CRA and to assess your suitability for the job to which you apply. The personal information collected through this website is described in Personal Information Bank (PIB) PSE 902, and will be retained for a minimum of two years from the most recent access or update. Your personal information is protected under the federal Privacy Act which states that you have the right to access your personal information and request changes to incorrect information.

Assessment results may also be collected and accessed in order to facilitate the staffing process.

The information you provide may also be collected, retained, used, or disclosed for analytical purposes by the CRA programs’ activities in accordance with the authority under the CRA Act.

If you have any questions or comments about our Privacy policy and practices and how we administer the Privacy Act, or if you are not satisfied that we have adequately respected your privacy, you are encouraged to contact the Access to Information and Privacy Directorate by email at ATIP@cra-arc.gc.caOpens a new email message or by telephone at 1-866-333-5402. You can also contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner at 1-800-282-1376.
