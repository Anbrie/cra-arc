Reporting tax exempt employment income

Pre-test question

Select True or False in your answer.

The employment income anybody earns on a reserve is tax exempt.

  • False

Only individuals with Status Indian can earn tax exempt employment income on a reserve.

Takeaway points

Steps to follow

  1. Review their background information and the required slips
  2. In the Interview setup, check the boxes next to Tax return for a Status Indian in the Specific situations section and Employment income and employment insurance benefits (T4, T4E/RL-6) in the Employment and other benefits section
  3. Click Status Indian in the left side menu and select Yes at the field, Please confirm that you are a registered Indian, or entitled to be registered as an Indian under the Indian Act
  4. Click T4 and employment income in the left side menu and click the + sign next to T4 income (earned in any province except Quebec).
  5. Complete the necessary fields. Select Exempt CPP/QPP (rare!) for field 28. Box 71 – Indian (exempt income) – Employment can be found in the drop-down menu under Other information


Even though Christie lives off reserve, her income is tax exempt because it was earned on reserve and she has Indian status. The software will automatically generate form T90 which shows her tax exempt income and allows Christie to include this income in the calculation of the Canada workers benefit (CWB).
