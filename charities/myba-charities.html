My business account for charities

My Business Account is a secure online portal that lets organizations access their Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) account information online.

There are many benefits of using My Business Account for charities:

  • Apply online to become a registered charity or a registered Canadian amateur athletic association
  • Submit a complete annual information return and required documentation, such as financial statements
  • Receive instant confirmation that your return has been filed. The date you submit your return through My Business Account is the date the CRA officially receives it
  • Update information about your organization, upload supporting documents, and correspond with the Charities Directorate
  • Access other related program accounts such as payroll and goods and services tax/harmonized sales tax

To use online services for charities through My Business Account, follow these steps:

Steps to access My Business Account for charities

  1. You will need a business number and a charity account

    Many organizations already have a business number (BN) and an associated charity account (RR) with the Canada Revenue Agency.

    Consider the following before requesting a new business number and RR account:

    • Is your organization already registered?

      Is your organization already a registered charity, a registered Canadian amateur athletic association, a registered national arts service organization or a registered head body?

      If yes, you are ready to go to Sign into My Business Account.

      Is your organization an internal division under a head body?

      If yes, you will need to confirm that your head body has a business number and an RR account. To access your RR account:

      1. Register for Represent a client to get a repID
      2. Your head body will then authorize you under their business number and your charity account

      For more information about internal divisions, visit Guidance CG-028, Head bodies and their internal divisions.

      Did your organization submit an application in the past?

      If your organization submitted an application in the past but was never registered, a business number and an associated RR account may have already been assigned.

      To complete a new application, contact the Charities Directorate. After confirming that you are authorized for the file, we will provide you with your business number and RR account, and restore your access to My Business Account.

      Is your organization trying to access My Business Account after having its charitable status revoked?

      If yes, when a charity or RCAAA's registration is revoked, it can no longer access its RR program account on My Business Account.

      To apply for re-registration, you need to contact the Charities Directorate. After confirming that you are authorized on the file, we will restore access to your organization’s RR account on My Business Account.

    If you answer no to all the questions above, go to Get your business number and charity account.

  2. Get your business number and charity account

    Business Registration Online is a self-serve application that lets you register for the following:

    • a business number (example: 123456789)
    • an associated charity program (RR) account (example: 123456789 RR 0001)
    • Sign into Business Registration Online
      1. Go to Business Registration Online, and click on "Register now"
      2. Enter your social insurance number (SIN)
      3. Enter your last name, date of birth, and postal code
      4. Read the terms and conditions, as well as the disclaimer
      5. Enter your phone information

    After you are signed in to Business Registration Online, choose one of the following options:

    • My organization has a business number and needs an associated charity account

      If your organization is incorporated or has another CRA program account (for example, payroll or GST/HST), then you already have a business number. Follow these instructions to add an RR program account to your business number:

      1. Select "I already have a business number"
      2. Enter your organization's business number, and select one of the following:
        • Business – Corporation (if your organization is incorporated)
        • Trust (if your organization is governed under a trust document)
        • Other (if your organization is governed under a constitution)
      3. If prompted, review/update the owner or business activity information
      4. Enter your legal name, corporation and owner/director information
      5. Select "Register CRA accounts for this business"
      6. Select "Register an RR account"
      7. Enter the RR program account language preference
      8. Select one of the following:
        • Charity
        • RCAAA (registered Canadian amateur athletic association)
        • RNASO (registered national arts service organization)
      9. Enter your mailing address, your physical location, and books and records addresses
      10. If you want to receive email notifications, add one or more email addresses
      11. After you've submitted your information, your business number and RR account number will be displayed on the following page
      12. Add additional owners to share access to your organization's RR account
      13. Select "Finished registering accounts with the CRA for this business"
      My organization needs a business number and a an RR account

      Follow these instructions to request a business number for your organization:

      1. Select "I need a business number"
      2. Select one of the following:
        • Business – Corporation (if your organization is incorporated)
        • Trust (if your organization is governed under a trust document)
        • Other (if your organization is governed under a constitution)
      3. Enter the first name and last name of the owner(s) (and SIN, if you selected "Trust" or "Other"), and their role and phone information
      4. Provide general information about your organization:
        • operation type
        • legal name and operating name
        • language preference
        • corporation information
        • email address
      5. Provide a mailing and physical address for your organization
      6. Enter the major business activity that describes your organization and the main products or services it provides

      Now that you created your business number, continue to request an associated RR account.

      1. Select "Register CRA accounts for this business"
      2. Select "Register an RR account"
      3. Enter the RR program account language preference
      4. Select one of the following:
        • Charity
        • RCAAA (registered Canadian amateur athletic association)
        • RNASO (registered national arts service organization)
      5. Enter your mailing address, your physical location, and books and records addresses
      6. If you want to receive email notifications, add one or more email addresses
      7. After you've submitted your information, your business number and RR account number will be displayed on the following page
      8. Add additional owners to share access to your organization's RR account
      9. Select "Finished registering accounts with the CRA for this business"

    A business number and RR account do not automatically make your organization a registered charity

    To become one, you will still need to submit an application to register a charity under the Income Tax Act.

    After you get a business number and associated RR account, your organization can start using online services for charities. Go to Sign into My Business Account.

  3. Sign into My Business Account

    If this is your first time using CRA digital services, you will need to register for a CRA sign-in account. Your CRA sign-in account gives you access to CRA's online services.

    1. Register for My Business Account

      Options to register for My Business Account, and what information you will need.

    2. Sign-in to My Business Account

      You will recieve your CRA security code within 5-10 business days, which is required to validate your registration. Once you have validated your registration you will be signed in to My Business Account.

    After you have signed into My Business Account, go to Access your business number through My Business Account.

  4. Access your business number through My Business Account

    Now that you have access to My Business Account, you’ll need to access your business number.

    Choose one of the following options:

    • If you are new to My Business Account
      1. Sign into My Business Account
      2. Enter your organization's business number and click on "Add"
      If you already use My Business Account to manage other businesses or organizations
      1. Sign into My Business Account
      2. Click on "Profile" at the top
      3. Select "Manage business number(s) in your profile"
      4. Click on "Add a business" to add your organization's business number
      5. Enter your organization's business number and click on "Add"
      6. After you are done, click on "My Business Account" to return to the Overview page
      If you don't see your organization's business number in My Business Account

      If you don’t see your organization’s business number after you signed into My Business Account, and you can’t add it to your profile, call the Charities Directorate at 1-800-267-2384. We will need your social insurance number to validate your identity before adding you as a director for your organization.
