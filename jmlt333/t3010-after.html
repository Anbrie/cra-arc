After you file

After you file your T3010 return, we will send you a confirmation of annual information return filing.

The information on your return will be available on the List of charities. You are responsible for making sure that your return is correct. If you notice an error, make sure to amend your return. Do not file another return for the same year.

Amending a return after you have filed

Online:

Sign in to My Business Account (MyBA) and select "Adjust a return".

Sign in to My Business Account Register

If you need help accessing MyBA or have questions about amending your return online, you can contact the Charities Directorate.

By mail or fax:
Amending your T3010 Return

If you are not able to adjust your T3010 Return online, you can file a paper version.

Complete Form 1240 Registered Charity Adjustment Request for each T3010 Return that you need to correct.

Make sure you enter the fiscal year-end on each form. The form must be signed by a representative of your charity that the Charities Directorate has on file.

Amending other forms filed with your T3010 Return

Complete Form 1240 Registered Charity Adjustment Request and attach the amended form (for example, Form T1235 or Form T1236).

Keep copies of Form T1240 and any revised forms with your books and records.

Mail or fax your amended return
