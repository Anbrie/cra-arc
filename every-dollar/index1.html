Every dollar counts

Raising children, working at minimum wage, living with disabilities, or just keeping up with the bills is not always easy. There are many benefits, credits, and deductions available to help you.

Featured

Canada child benefit (CCB)

Provides monthly tax-free money. Get up to $6,997/year for each child under 6 and up to $5,903/year, for each child aged 6 to 17.

Canada workers benefit (CWB)

Provides $500 to those in the workforce and earning a low income. Part is payable in advance.

GST/HST credit

Provides 4 tax-free payments per year. Single individuals can get up to $467/year. A couple with two children can receive up to $934/year.

Disability tax credit (DTC)

Provides a tax deduction to people with impairments, or a supporting family member. An individual can claim up to $8,662, with an additional $5,053 for those under 18.
