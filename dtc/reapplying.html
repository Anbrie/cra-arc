Keep getting the credit

Once you are approved for the credit, the notice of determination will show which year(s) you are eligible for the DTC. You do not need to send a new Form T2201 each year, unless we tell you that we need one.

You can view your DTC information in My Account.

You should tell us if your medical condition improves and you no longer meet the criteria for the DTC.
