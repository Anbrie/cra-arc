Create candidate profile

Your candidate profile allows you to apply for job opportunities at the CRA. The profile consists of information about yourself that is important to our hiring procedures. The profile process is divided into 7 steps:

  • Step 1 - Personal data
  • Step 2 - Addresses
  • Step 3 - Contact information
  • Step 4 - Correspondence preferences
  • Step 5 - Education
  • Step 6 - Work experience
  • Step 7 - Veterans
  • Step 8 - Profile release

Once your profile is complete, you will be able to navigate to the individual steps directly without having to start from step 1.
