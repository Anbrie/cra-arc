Remit (pay) payroll deductions and contributions

You have to register for a payroll program account before the first remittance due date. If you do not have a payroll account, see Open or manage a payroll account.

If you do not open a payroll account before you hire employees, you must still calculate deductions and pay them by the due date. If this is not done, you may be assessed a penalty.

Business in Quebec

Business in Quebec

If you have a place of business in Quebec, you may have to deduct provincial income tax, Quebec Pension Plan contributions, and Quebec Parental Insurance Plan (QPIP) premiums and remit these to Revenue Quebec.
