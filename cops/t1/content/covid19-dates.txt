  <h2 class="h5"><span class="nowrap">COVID-19:</span> Important updates</h2>    
  <ul>    
    <li><a href="https://www.canada.ca/content/canadasite/en/revenue-agency/services/benefits/apply-for-cerb-with-cra.html">Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB)</a>: Apply for the CERB using CRA My Account or over the phone.</li>    
    <li><a href="https://www.canada.ca/content/canadasite/en/revenue-agency/services/subsidy/emergency-wage-subsidy.html">Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy (CEWS)</a>: Apply using CRA My Business Account, Represent a Client or the Web Forms application.</a>   
</li>   
<li><a href="https://www.canada.ca/content/canadasite/en/revenue-agency/services/benefits/emergency-student-benefit.html">Canada Emergency Student Benefit (CESB)</a>: Apply using CRA My Account or over the phone.</li>   
    <li><a href="https://www.canada.ca/content/canadasite/en/revenue-agency/campaigns/covid-19-update/covid-19-filing-payment-dates.html">Tax filing and payment deadlines for 2019</a>: The deadline for individuals to file was extended to <strong class="nowrap">June 1, 2020</strong>, and the deadline to pay any amounts owed has been further extended to <strong class="nowrap">September 30, 2020</strong>. Note: some taxpayers may have received a Notice of Assessment that says the deadline for payment is <span class="nowrap">April 30, 2020</span> or <span class="nowrap">September 1, 2020</span>, which is incorrect.</li>    
     </ul>    
  <p class="mrgn-tp-md">Find <a href="https://www.canada.ca/content/canadasite/en/revenue-agency/campaigns/covid-19-update.html">more information on tax and benefits measures</a> to help support Canadians during the COVID-19 pandemic.</p>    
</section>
