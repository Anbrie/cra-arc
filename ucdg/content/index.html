Consider the needs of the audience

The user can be anyone, which means you must be aware of various audience needs. This awareness will help you develop empathy for users. This is different than sympathy, which only acknowledges the challenges. Empathy allows you to relate to users, so you can create content that solves problems, supports comprehension, and improves task success.

Start with the most important information

Start your content with the most important information, and be direct. When people scan web pages, they tend to start in the top left hand corner and scan to the right and down. As they move down the page, they scan less and less to the right.

Use the right tone and voice

The tone of the government's web content should be direct, informative, conversational, and professional. Write as if you were talking to your audience in person, but with the authority of an institution that provides information as part of its mandate.
