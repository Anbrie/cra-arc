Line 42200 - Social benefits repayment

Note: Line 42200 was line 422 before tax year 2019.

If you received employment insurance (EI) benefits, old age security (OAS) pension, net federal supplements, and/or the Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB) during the year, you may have to repay all or part of your social benefits. See the information at line 23500 to calculate your repayment.

Completing your tax return

Enter on line 42200 of your return the social benefits repayment amount from line 23500 of your return.
