Every dollar counts

Raising children, working at minimum wage, living with disabilities, buying or renovating a home, or just keeping up with the bills is not always easy. There are many benefits, credits, and programs available to help you.

Featured

Canada child benefit (CCB)

Monthly tax-free money. Over one year, families can get up to $6,997 for each child under 6 and up to $5,903 for each child aged 6 to 17.

Canada workers benefit (CWB)

A refundable tax credit to help those working and earning a low income. If eligible, get up to half of the CWB in advance payments.

GST/HST credit

A tax-free amount paid four times every year. Over one year, single individuals can get up to $467 and a couple with two children can receive up to $934.

Disability tax credit (DTC)

A non-refundable tax credit that can offer relief to people with impairments, or their supporting family member, by lowering the amount of income tax owed. Eligible individuals or a supporting person may be able to claim up to $8,662, with an additional $5,053 for an eligible dependant who is under 18.

Home Buyers’ Plan

Withdraw up to $35,000 from your registered retirement savings plan (RRSPs) to buy or build a home.
