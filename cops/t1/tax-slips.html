When you will receive your tax slips

You should have received most of your slips and receipts by the end of February. However, you may not receive your T3 and T5013 slips until the end of March.

Request a copy of a tax slip

You can get a copy of your current and prior year tax slips if you have not received it, or it is missing or lost.

  • T4E Statement of Employment Insurance and Other Benefits
  • T4RIF Statement of income from a Registered Retirement Income Fund
