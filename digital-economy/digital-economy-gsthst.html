Simplified GST/HST for non-resident digital businesses

The government announced proposed GST/HST measures for non-resident digital businesses who carry on business in Canada. This change would affect businesses who take part in cross-border digital products and services, have sales of goods located in Canada or run platform-based short-term accommodations.

If this applies to your business, you will pay a simplified GST/HST instead of the normal GST/HST. A simplified GST/HST means you are not entitled to claim input tax credits, but you have to report your income and charge the tax.

If approved by Parliament, these new measures will come into effect on July 1, 2021.
