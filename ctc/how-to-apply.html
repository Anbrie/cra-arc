How to claim

You can claim the Canada training credit when you complete your income tax and benefit return

The Canada training credit (CTC) claimed on line 45350 of your income tax and benefit return will reduce your tax owing.

Electronic filing: Option 1 of 2

Follow the instructions in your certified tax software.

Paper filing: Option 2 of 2

If you file a paper return, complete Schedule 11, Federal Tuition, Education, and Textbook Amounts and Canada Training Credit, in your income tax package.
