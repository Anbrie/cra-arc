Eligibility - COVID-19 benefits from CRA for individuals

COVID-19 benefits from CRA for individuals

Status: All benefits are closed

The CRA administered COVID-19 benefits to provide temporary income support to individuals between March 15, 2020 and May 7, 2022. Review each benefit’s eligibility criteria and application periods to validate your application or find out how to repay your benefit overpayments.

Services and information

Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB) Closed

CERB was available from March 15, 2020 to September 26, 2020, the last day to apply retroactively was December 2, 2020

Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB) Closed

CRB was available from September 27, 2020 to October 23, 2021, the last day to apply retroactively was December 22, 2021

Canada Recovery Caregiving Benefit (CRCB) Closed

CRCB was available from September 27, 2020 to May 7, 2022, the last day to apply retroactively was July 6, 2022

Canada Recovery Sickness Benefit (CRSB) Closed

CRSB was available from September 27, 2020 to May 7, 2022, the last day to apply retroactively was on July 6, 2022

Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit (CWLB) Closed

CWLB was available from October 24, 2021 to May 7, 2022, the last day to apply retroactively was May 18, 2022

Canada Emergency Student Benefit (CESB) Closed

CESB was available from May 10, 2020 to August 29, 2020, the last day to apply retroactively was September 30, 2020
