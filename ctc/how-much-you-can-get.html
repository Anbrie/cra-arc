How much you can get

The amount you can claim for the CTC is whichever amount is less:

Your Canada training credit limit (CTCL)  for the tax year

50% of the eligible tuition and other fees paid to an eligible educational institution in Canada for courses you took in the year, or fees paid to certain bodies for an occupational, trade or professional exam taken in the year

Canada training credit limit (CTCL)

Your CTCL will be shared with you each year on your latest notice of assessment or reassessment and will be available through the Canada Revenue Agency’s My Account for Individuals.

If the credit is more than your tax owing, you may get a refund for the difference.
