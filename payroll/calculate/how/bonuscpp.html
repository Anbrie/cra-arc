Calculations of bonuses, retroactive payments or irregular amounts

In addition to the regular types of payments such as salary, wages, etc., you may pay employees other types of payments. For example:

  • Bonus (for example, an incentive payment made at the end of a fiscal or calendar year)
  • Retroactive pay (for example, a backdated increase in pay after a new contract is finalized)
  • Other types of irregular payments

Determine the CPP contributions to withhold

You have to deduct CPP contributions from bonuses and retroactive pay increases.

If the payment is made separately from regular pay, no exemption is included and you must use one of the following methods:

  • Bonus method
  • Formulas for employers using software programs

Bonus method - How to calculate

The bonus method does not take into consideration the basic exemption.
