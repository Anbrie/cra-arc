How much you can get

On this page

  • Applicant is 18 or older
  • Applicant is 17 or younger

Applicant is 18 or older

If you qualify for the credit and were 18 years of age or older at the end of 2018, the federal impacted health amount of $8,235 can be claimed on line 316 of Schedule 1.

Applicant is 17 or younger

If you qualify for the impacted health amount and were under 18 years of age at the end of the year, you can claim up to an additional $4,804. This supplement may be reduced if, in 2018, one of the following situations applies:

  • Someone claimed child care expenses for you on line 214 of their tax return or attendant care expenses for you on line 330 or line 331 of Schedule 1.
  • You claimed attendant care expenses on line 215 of your tax return or on line 330 of Schedule 1.
To calculate this supplement, use the Worksheet for Schedule 1.
